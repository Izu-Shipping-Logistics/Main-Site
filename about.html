Timeline
No Flex
1996
Founder Joins Bhagwati Shipping
Founder Joins Bhagwati Shipping
In 1996, the CEO of IZU Shipping & Logistics embarked on a pivotal journey by joining Bhagwati Shipping as the Import & Export Operations Manager. This role marked the beginning of a career dedicated to the intricate processes and dynamic challenges of the logistics industry.
Specialization in Refer Container Cargo
2008
Specialization in Refer Container Cargo
By 2008, IZU Shipping & Logistics had honed its expertise in handling specialized refer container cargo. This specialization allowed the company to ensure the safe and efficient transportation of perishable goods, meeting the unique demands of clients across diverse industries.
2008
Management of Unused & Old Cargo
Management of Unused & Old Cargo
During this period, IZU Shipping & Logistics successfully navigated the complexities of managing shipments of unused and old cargo. With meticulous attention to detail and strategic planning, the company facilitated the transportation and disposition of cargo, contributing to seamless logistics operations.
Handling Special Import Shipment
2009
Handling Special Import Shipment
In 2009, IZU Shipping & Logistics undertook the responsibility of managing special import shipments for Mumbai Port Trust. This endeavor showcased the company's commitment to handling high-stakes logistics projects, ensuring the timely and secure delivery of critical imports.
2012
Co-Founding AS Clearing & Logistics (ASCL)
Co-Founding AS Clearing & Logistics (ASCL)
The year 2012 marked a significant milestone for IZU Shipping & Logistics with the co-founding of AS Clearing & Logistics (ASCL). This strategic partnership firm expanded the company's reach and capabilities, allowing for the provision of comprehensive logistics solutions to a broader clientele.
Licensed Custom Broker
2015
Licensed Custom Broker
In 2015, IZU Shipping & Logistics achieved a significant milestone by registering AS Clearing & Logistics (ASCL) as a licensed custom broker with Indian Customs. This accreditation underscored the company's commitment to regulatory compliance and enhanced its ability to facilitate seamless import and export processes for clients.
2015
Successful Import Shipments for RCF Ltd
Successful Import Shipments for RCF Ltd
During the same period, IZU Shipping & Logistics successfully managed import shipments for RCF Ltd, a prominent government undertaking in India. These import operations highlighted the company's proficiency in navigating complex logistics requirements and ensuring the timely delivery of critical goods for its esteemed clients.
Expertise in EPCG Scheme
2016
Expertise in EPCG Scheme
By 2016, IZU Shipping & Logistics had developed expertise in handling imports of old and used capital goods under the EPCG (Export Promotion Capital Goods) scheme. This specialization allowed the company to assist clients in availing duty benefits and complying with regulatory obligations associated with capital goods imports.
2018
Diverse Cargo Handling
Diverse Cargo Handling
In 2018, IZU Shipping & Logistics demonstrated its versatility by successfully managing various types of cargo, including re-export and re-import shipments, as well as hazardous and non-hazardous cargo. This diverse portfolio showcased the company's ability to adapt to different logistical challenges and deliver tailored solutions to meet client needs.
Operational Excellence in ASCL
2019
Operational Excellence in ASCL
The year 2019 marked a significant milestone for AS Clearing & Logistics (ASCL), with the achievement of over 700 successful jobs. This milestone underscored the company's commitment to operational excellence, customer satisfaction, and the seamless execution of logistics services.
2021
Handling Complex ODC Cargo
Handling Complex ODC Cargo
In 2021, IZU Shipping & Logistics demonstrated its capability by successfully managing the transportation of complex ODC (Over Dimensional Cargo) cargo, including ISO tanks, for Seven Star Engineers & Fabricators. This accomplishment highlighted the company's expertise in handling specialized cargo and executing complex logistics operations with precision.
Reestablishment of IZU Shipping & Logistics
2023
Reestablishment of IZU Shipping & Logistics
The year 2023 marked a pivotal moment as IZU Shipping & Logistics was reestablished, reaffirming its commitment to excellence and innovation in the logistics industry. This milestone signaled a new chapter in the company's journey, poised to continue delivering exceptional logistics solutions to its valued clients.
